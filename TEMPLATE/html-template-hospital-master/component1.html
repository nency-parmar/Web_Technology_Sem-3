Acne


Acne is a skin condition that may occur when the hair follicles become clogged with dead skin and oily skin so it will further result in whiteheads, blackheads, or pimples.

The problem of acne is very common among teenagers, but it is found among people of all ages. There are several acne treatments but the bumps will take time to go flat, and others may pop up very soon. Acne also causes emotional distress as they are very clearly visible on the face.



Our treatment

Depending on the severity, acne will take time to treat. The earlier you start the treatment, the sooner it cures. Whiteheads, blackheads, small red, tender bumps, pimples, are all symptoms of acne and you may not miss going for treatment. If acnes are not treated with primary care you must visit our dermatologist for better treatment. Our treatment involves prescribing you creams and ointments that will provide you instant relief. We will also prescribe you vitamin supplements to reduce the chances of acne occurring again. In older adults, acne may also be a symptom of an underlying disease so it is required to visit the best dermatologist.
